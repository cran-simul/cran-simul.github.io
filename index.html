SiMul Research group @ CRAN

Multidimensional signal processing in Nancy, France

We are a research group part of the BioSiS department at CRAN, Nancy. We develop novel theoretical and methodological tools for the processing of multidimensional data, putting together mathematical concepts, physical modeling and experimental data processing.
